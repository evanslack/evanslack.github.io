THE ETHICS OF WAR - RESULTS OF PLAY


Here lie the results of the war:

Country one is destroyed, its economy in shambles, new wars springing up in war-ravaged municipalities, which makes it easy for Country two to eat country one becoming larger and more powerful. Though the US alliance with country two lasts for several decades, once country two grows large enough to be considered an empire, the alliance is broken and country two wages war upon the US. The US military is no match for the empire that country two has become, and eventually the US becomes another extension of country two.
